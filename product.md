# Product Logs (product.md)

## 2026-03-27
### Milestones
- **P0 Pivot:** Shifted core objective from "Trading/Execution" to "Institutional Research & Brand Building." Focus is now on creating sellable, high-quality reports.
- **Source Nuance:** Re-defined `research_inbox` as a "Source of Emphasis" rather than a closed-loop context. Broadened scope to include MCP and web-search APIs.
- **Persona Alignment:** Mapped 8 specific professional personas to the Council to ensure diverse but institutional-grade outputs.

### Lessons
- **Brand over Ops:** For a solo founder with limited capital, the brand value of high-quality research is a better "P0" than the operational complexity of a full-stack fund.
- **The "Multi-Sourced" Philosophy:** Moving away from a single source of truth (the inbox) to a "trusted emphasis + broad scanning" model improves the robustness and credibility of the final research product.
- **Build in Public:** Committed to radical transparency to build brand trust with potential clients from day one.
